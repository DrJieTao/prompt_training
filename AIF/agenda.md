# VC Efficiency Accelerator: A 60-Minute AI Intensive Walk-Along

**Designed For:** VC Investors (GPs, Associates, Platform/Operating Partners)  
**Format:** High-Impact 60-Minute Workshop (40m Live Demos + 20m Strategic Q&A)

---

## Executive Value Propositions

- **Deal Velocity:** Move from raw investment notes to a polished LP update deck in under 10 minutes, reclaiming hours for sourcing and relationship-building.
- **Administrative Clarity:** Transform messy IC notes and board meeting takeaways into professional, visual handouts instantly.
- **Competitive Edge:** Master the AI tools reshaping how top-tier funds operate—before your portfolio companies ask you about them.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Master Meta-Prompting:** Learn the **RTCF Formula** (Role, Task, Context, Format) to use AI as a "Co-Pilot" rather than a chatbot.
2. **Visualize Workflow:** Turn rough text into professional diagrams for IC presentations and board decks.
3. **Synthesize Research:** Preview how AI can instantly query dozens of pitch decks and market reports simultaneously.

## Pathways to Mastery: Follow-Up Workshops

To ensure sustainable adoption, we propose a tiered follow-up series:

### Workshop A: The Responsible Investor (60m)
Dedicated session on **Data Defense**, protecting deal confidentiality, "Trust but Verify" techniques, and mitigating hallucination risks in high-stakes outputs.

### Workshop B: The Due Diligence Architect (60m)
Deep-dive into **NotebookLM** and research synthesis tools for querying large document sets (pitch decks, market reports, technical documentation).

### Workshop C: Portfolio Enablement (60m)
Training investors to cascade AI literacy to portfolio companies, establishing the fund as a value-add partner for operational efficiency.
