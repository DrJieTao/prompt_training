# Library Efficiency Accelerator: A 60-Minute AI Intensive Walk-Along
**Designed For:** All Library Staff (Reference, Operations, Marketing, Admin)  
**Format:** High-Impact 60-Minute Workshop (45m Live Demos + 15m Strategic Q&A)

---

### **Executive Value Propositions**
*   **Operational Velocity:** Move from a program concept to a finished proposal and presentation in under 5 minutes, reclaiming hours for community-facing work.
*   **Administrative Clarity:** Transform messy meeting notes and verbal workflows into professional, visual SOPs instantly.
*   **Community Leadership:** Demonstrate the Library’s role as an **Innovation Hub**, mastering the tools that are reshaping the information landscape.

### **Learning Objectives**
1.  **Master Meta-Prompting:** Learn to use AI as a "Co-Pilot" to design tasks before executing them.
2.  **Visualize Workflow:** Turn rough text into professional diagrams for internal training and board reports.
3.  **Synthesize Knowledge:** Experience how AI can instantly summarize complex library documents or local history collections.

---

### **The "Showcase & Follow-Along" Demos**

**1. The "Program-in-a-Box" (Follow-Along)**
*   **The Flow:** Use a **Meta-Prompt** in ChatGPT/Claude to brainstorm a new library series (e.g., "AI for Seniors"), then push that brief into **Gamma**.
*   **The Result:** A professional landing page and promotional slide deck created in seconds.
*   **Impact:** Marketing and Program Management.

**2. The "Workflow Wizard" (Follow-Along)**
*   **The Flow:** Take raw, bulleted notes from a staff meeting and use **Napkin.ai** to turn them into a professional, visual SOP flowchart.
*   **The Result:** A visual "how-to" guide ready for the staff breakroom or the board report.
*   **Impact:** Operations, Admin, and Workflow Analysis.

**3. The "Instant Scholar" (Quick Demo)**
*   **The Flow:** A 5-minute showcase of **NotebookLM**.
*   **The Result:** Watch as a stack of local history PDFs or long policy manuals are instantly turned into a searchable, synthesized "Study Guide" for reference librarians.
*   **Impact:** Reference Support and Deep Research.

---

### **The "Efficient 60" Agenda**
*   **00:00 – 00:05:** **The Innovation Hub Vision.** Framing AI as a tool for staff empowerment.
*   **00:05 – 00:35:** **Follow-Along Demos.** Real-time execution of Case 1 (Gamma) and Case 2 (Napkin.ai).
*   **00:35 – 00:40:** **Quick Demo.** The power of NotebookLM for reference and policy.
*   **00:40 – 00:45:** **The "Monday-Ready" Cheat Sheet.** Quick intro to the RTCF formula.
*   **00:45 – 01:00:** **Discussion & Q&A.** Focusing on immediate departmental use cases.

---

### **Pathways to Mastery: Follow-Up Workshops**
To ensure sustainable adoption, we propose a tiered follow-up series:

1.  **Workshop A: The Responsible Librarian (60m)**
    *   Dedicated session on **Data Defense**, protecting Patron PII, "Trust but Verify" verification techniques, and the ethics of AI in public spaces.
2.  **Workshop B: The Reference Architect (60m)**
    *   Deep-dive into **NotebookLM** and specialized research tools for synthesizing localized history and complex patron inquiries.
3.  **Workshop C: Community Outreach (60m)**
    *   Training staff to lead "AI 101" sessions for library patrons, establishing the library as the community's primary resource for AI literacy.
